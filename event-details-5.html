Living in the IT Era

Ms. Claudine Lumactud

Digital technology is the most powerful and tangible tool we currently have available to exploit the ensuring opportunities for social change. This course will examine the communication and information technologies with particular emphasis on the IC3 Digital Literacy Certification. The course was designed to help the students get the most out of information technology that has been made in IC3 GS5. It includes the concepts and skills that apply to almost any school or career pathway to enhance and validate their digital skills. The course will cover three major areas of IC3: Computing Fundamentals, Key Applications, and Living On-line.
